Sitewide Pages Design Harmonization

Date: 2026-05-15

Goal: bring the rest of the site closer to the visual language of История и кодекс.

What was wrong

  • The history page had a stronger, more premium visual system than the rest of the product.
  • Other page heroes felt flatter and more generic.
  • Cards across news, missions, studios, leaderboard, and placeholder pages did not feel like one family.
  • Desktop and mobile rhythm varied too much from page to page.

What was changed

  • Introduced a shared ceremonial-page wrapper for non-history pages.
  • Upgraded hero blocks with richer gradients, stronger shadows, and an inner frame.
  • Unified card treatment for home, missions, approve, studios, news forms, and leaderboard shells.
  • Improved centering and composition for card content and actions.
  • Added mobile-specific ceremonial tuning so the design holds together on small screens.

Pages affected

  • Главная
  • Таблица лидеров
  • Новости
  • Предложенные новости
  • Миссии за валюту
  • Подтверждение
  • Студии
  • Placeholder pages

Note

Lazyweb MCP references were not available in this session, so this report is based on a local design audit plus direct implementation in the codebase.
